Learn the most in-demand skills

React is the most sought-after front-end framework. We'll teach you to use it in-depth, but we don't stop there. We'll introduce you to its key peripheral technologies, and teach you all about testing front-end applications.

Learn to use in-demand technologies professionally across the front and back end of applications, including React, Node, Express, SQL and NoSQL. You’ll be trained in industry best practice and standards, and we will help you to get a foothold in the tech industry! Frequently Asked Questions

Our Head Office and in-person teaching hub is located at 50 Main Street, Notting Hill, in Central London. We offer fully virtual and hybrid learning models to suit each individual.

The total cost of our coding bootcamp is £5500. The main 15-week coding bootcamp costs £5000, and the introductory week costs £500.

If after the induction week you do not want to continue progressing onto the core bootcamp, you will receive your coding bootcamp fees back in full, no questions asked.

There are many finance plans available to our students, we work with FCA-regulated companies who can provide alternative ways of covering your course fees.

Furthermore, we have an Advance payment option, where you pay 1 month of your course fees upfront and get a lower 16-week bootcamp cost of £4500 (ususally £5500). Another option available to our students is the Deferred payment option, this allows the fees to be paid back, interest free, over the course of 24 months.
